### Uninstalling
1. Open FBI
1. Go to `Titles`
1. Find two `TWiLight Menu++` titles in purple text
1. Delete both titles
1. Exit the title list
1. Go to the SD card
1. Open `_nds`
1. Delete the `TWiLightMenu` folder
1. Delete the nds-bootstrap and GBARunner2 files
1. Exit `_nds`
1. Delete `boot.nds`
